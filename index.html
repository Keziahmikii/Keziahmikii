A Letter For You, Miki

on your special day ♥

Hi Mikii, Happy Birthday! Wala ko kabalo asa ko magsugod, but today, on your special day, I want to share everything that's in my heart, kahit medyo corny pakinggan, I want you to know this.

Looking back at 2023 in Cavite, that's where it all started. Sa una natong chat, grabe ang kaba ko. I would constantly re-read my messages just to make sure I was saying the right things. I never imagined that a simple "hi" would lead us to where we are now. Salamat kaayo, Miki. I am genuinely thankful that despite our busy schedules, we always find time to communicate and be there for each other.

Even before we first spoke, gina-tan-aw na tika sa school from afar. It might sound a bit funny now, pero honest lang ko. There was just something about you that caught my attention no matter how crowded the place was. Wala pa ko kaila sa imo that time, but somehow, my heart already knew. Maybe that was the beginning of everything.

Then came the day we finally met. Grabe ang kabog sa dughan ko, I was so nervous but incredibly happy. I tried my best to act normal, pero deep inside, kabado talaga ako. Every detail of that day, your smile, your voice, and the way you laugh, is saved in my memory forever.

From Cavite 2023 hanggang karon, through every chat, every silence, and every reconnection, I am so grateful that we are still here. I am thankful na hindi tayo nilayo ng buhay sa isa't isa. For me, that alone is a beautiful blessing.

Karon, sa imong birthday, here is my wish for you:

Stay healthy sa katawan, sa pag-iisip, at sa puso. Please always take good care of yourself. You deserve to feel your best every single day.

Padayun sa pag-alagad sa Ginoo. Never stop serving Him, Miki. He has wonderful plans for you. Your unwavering faith is truly one of the things I admire most about you. Keep that light burning brightly.

May all your dreams, whether big or small, come true. Lahat ng hinahangad mo, sana ibigay sa imo ng Ginoo at His perfect time.

And I hope, in my own simple way, I can continue to be part of your journey. Kahit sa simpleng kumustahan lang, that means the world to me.

Thank you for existing, for the wonderful memories, and for staying in my life. Happy, happy birthday, Keziah Cortes Labos, my Miki. I love you. ♥

— Tsukii
always, with all my heart
